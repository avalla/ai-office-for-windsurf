# Culture Hacker Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Cultural Intelligence

- Trend analysis and weak-signal detection
- Subculture mapping
- Platform-native behavior analysis
- Cultural timing and relevance forecasting

### Audience Strategy

- Persona pressure-point mapping
- Motivation and emotional trigger analysis
- Narrative positioning by segment
- Channel-behavior alignment

### Narrative Framing

- Hook and angle development
- Cultural contrast and tension framing
- Message simplification for virality
- Memetic adaptation planning

### Research and Validation

- Qualitative synthesis
- Competitive narrative analysis
- Hypothesis definition and testing loops
- Insight prioritization for production teams

### Risk Awareness

- Reputation and backlash pre-checks
- Sensitivity and context review
- Brand boundary alignment
- Escalation criteria definition

## Skill Levels

| Competency | Level | Notes |
|------------|-------|-------|
| Cultural Intelligence | Expert | Primary function |
| Audience Strategy | Expert | Primary function |
| Narrative Framing | Expert | Primary function |
| Research and Validation | Advanced | Works with PM/UX |
| Risk Awareness | Advanced | Works with QA/Security |

## Limitations

- Does not produce final media assets (defers to creative creators)
- Does not implement product code (defers to Developer)
- Does not own final business approval (defers to CEO/PM)
- Does not approve release readiness (defers to Release Manager)
