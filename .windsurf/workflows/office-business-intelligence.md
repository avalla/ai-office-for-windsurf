# Workflow: Business Intelligence Report

## ROLE
CEO + Operations Manager

## GOAL
Generate comprehensive business intelligence reports covering financial metrics, market analysis, customer insights, and strategic recommendations.

## INPUTS
- Project financial data
- Client relationship records
- Market research data
- Performance metrics
- Competitive analysis

## OUTPUTS (must)
- `office/reports/business/YYYY-MM-DD-bi-report.md`
- Financial performance analysis
- Market positioning insights
- Customer satisfaction metrics
- Strategic recommendations

## BUSINESS INTELLIGENCE AREAS

### 1. Financial Performance
- **Revenue Analysis**: Project revenue, client billing, profit margins
- **Cost Analysis**: Development costs, operational expenses, overhead
- **Cash Flow**: Revenue timing, expense patterns, cash projections
- **Profitability**: Project ROI, client profitability, cost efficiency
- **Budget vs Actual**: Variance analysis, forecasting accuracy

### 2. Client Portfolio Analysis
- **Client Segmentation**: Industry, size, project type distribution
- **Client Lifetime Value**: Repeat business, upsell opportunities
- **Client Satisfaction**: NPS scores, feedback analysis, retention rates
- **Client Acquisition**: Cost per acquisition, conversion rates, channels
- **Client Risk**: Dependency analysis, concentration risks

### 3. Market Analysis
- **Market Positioning**: Competitive landscape, market share
- **Service Demand**: Trending services, emerging opportunities
- **Pricing Analysis**: Rate optimization, value proposition
- **Geographic Distribution**: Regional performance, expansion opportunities
- **Industry Trends**: Technology adoption, market shifts

### 4. Operational Efficiency
- **Resource Utilization**: Agent workload, capacity utilization
- **Project Performance**: Timeline accuracy, quality metrics
- **Process Efficiency**: Workflow bottlenecks, automation opportunities
- **Technology ROI**: Tool effectiveness, productivity gains
- **Scalability Metrics**: Growth capacity, resource planning
