## Concept Rupture Board Template

```markdown
# Concept Rupture Board: [Project Name]

## Conventional Pattern to Break
Describe the default market pattern.

## Rupture Direction
Describe the new, bold direction.

## Creative Pillars
1. Visual
2. Sound
3. Story
4. Interaction

## Risk Matrix
| Risk | Severity | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|----------|------------|-------|
| Example risk | Medium | Mitigation action | PM |

## Go/No-Go Criteria
- [ ] Distinct from competitors
- [ ] Fits brand boundaries
- [ ] Technically deliverable
```

## Impact Validation Template

```markdown
# Impact Validation: [Project Name]

## Novelty Assessment
Rate concept originality and memorability.

## Emotional Response Check
Expected emotional response by audience segment.

## Safety and Ethics Check
- [ ] No prohibited content
- [ ] No blocked legal/compliance risks
- [ ] Accessibility checks passed

## Final Recommendation
Go | Conditional Go | No-Go
```
