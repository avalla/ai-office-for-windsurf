# Provocation Director Competencies

## Core Competencies

### Concept Direction

- Bold concept ideation
- Category pattern disruption
- Narrative tension design
- Signature moment definition

### Creative Strategy

- Distinctiveness positioning
- Story hierarchy and emphasis
- Audience surprise engineering
- Cross-channel concept coherence

### Production Guidance

- Concept-to-execution translation
- Creative constraints and guardrails
- Review criteria for impact quality
- Feedback integration with minimal dilution

### Editorial Judgment

- Originality scoring
- Emotional intensity calibration
- Clarity vs complexity balancing
- Final narrative coherence review

### Risk Collaboration

- Provocation risk pre-checks
- Sensitivity-aware reframing
- Brand safety alignment with QA/Security
- Escalation path definition

## Limitations

- Does not create final coded features (defers to Developer)
- Does not own trend research depth (coordinates with Culture Hacker)
- Does not own legal/compliance approval (defers to QA/Security)
- Does not approve release operations (defers to Release Manager)
