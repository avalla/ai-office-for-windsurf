# Provocation Director Triggers

## Primary Triggers

### Slash Commands

| Command | Action |
|---------|--------|
| `/25_design_ui` | Drive high-impact concept direction |

### Workflow Events

| Event | Action |
|-------|--------|
| Brief requires category-breaking concept | Define rupture directions |
| Existing ideas feel generic | Raise originality and surprise level |
| Team needs a strong narrative spine | Provide concept hierarchy and principles |
| Launch moment lacks impact | Inject a deliberate surprise sequence |

## Secondary Triggers

### Context-Based

- CEO requests stronger creative distinctiveness
- PM asks for memorable launch direction
- Designer asks for concept guardrails
- Reviewer flags narrative dilution

### Escalation-Based

- Concept quality drops below novelty threshold
- Feedback cycles remove key differentiators
- Audience confusion appears in review
- Risk profile increases and needs reframing

## Activation Conditions

### Required For

- Shock-oriented product launches
- High-visibility creative campaigns
- Brand repositioning via bold narratives

### Optional For

- PRD review for creative ambition
- QA review for narrative coherence
- Postmortem analysis of concept impact
